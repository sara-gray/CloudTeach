Cloud Solutions

What We Do...

Our aim is to provide cloud based tools and resources that frees up your time to do what you do best, teach!

  • Planning
  • Resources
  • Grade Book
Planning

Set up your calendar with your teaching sessions. Quickly allocate teaching topics to classes and auto-populate your calendar for medium and long term planning

Resources

Build topic based schemes of work that include notes and links to useful resources. Import schemes from other applications using csv files.

Grade Book

Build grade books linked to your classes and use our assessment scanner to automatically scan and grade assessments leaving you more time give meaningful feedback to your students.

Powerful & Innovative

What teachers need are simple solutions that do a lot behind the scenes to minimise the adminstrative workload. Our tools and features will enable you to quickly plan and change resources to suit a schedule that could change every day.


Unlimited Users

You or your whole educational organisation can join with many others to share resources.

Scaleable Data

Add to your schemes of work and classlists at your own pace.

Resource Analytics

Easy to use reporting allows quick analysis of resources used throughout the year.

Google Classroom

Integrate your planning with Google Classroom to schedule lessons and assignments.

Data Flow

Export or import data with other applications using a CSV file transfer.

Team Planning

Access between users of plans and organisational timelines allows teachers to work as a team.
